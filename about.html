About Us

Background

This is a non-profit organization whose aim is to save this generation so as to preserve the future through love, care and concern by providing fees and skilling to the needy in various areas when spotted. It targets at taking care of the needy in their homes. We plan to deliver entrepreneur training to the women to build their confidence empower them with lifesaving skills and provide practical business skills so that after completion of the projects, mothers and care-takers can be able to provide for their children and supplement their income.
